### Description _(998 / 1000 chars — fits the form's character limit)_
```
Imagine being a Malaysian founder filing your company taxes with zero knowledge of the law — like solving quantum theorems without knowing basic physics. Malaysian corporate tax is built for accountants, not founders. CukaiPandai fixes that. From one upload of your financials, you get three things: every deadline on one calendar, a computed Form C, and a draft filing pack, every ringgit traced to Malaysian law.

A deterministic rules engine in Python does all the tax math and citations, so no AI decides a figure and nothing is hallucinated. The AI only reads and classifies documents; a citation gate throws out any fake law. It runs on ILMU, Malaysia's own LLM by YTL AI Labs, so data stays in-country.

Bukku and other tools stop at the tax line; chatbots make the law up. CukaiPandai computes your Form C like a real tax agent, backing every figure with law you can defend. One upload. Zero hallucinations. Every ringgit cited. That's CukaiPandai — a pandai solution for your cukai problems.
```
